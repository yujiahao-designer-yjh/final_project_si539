Adoption Process

A simple, clear guide to help you bring your new pet home.

How It Works

We've made the process as welcoming and straightforward as possible.

01

Browse Available Pets

Start by exploring our Available Pets page. Filter by cats or dogs and read each pet's profile to find one that matches your lifestyle and energy level.

02

Submit an Adoption Application

Fill out our Adoption Application Form. It asks about your living situation, experience with pets, and why you'd like to adopt. Takes about 10 minutes.

03

Application Review

Our team reviews your application within 24–48 hours. We look for a good match — not a perfect record. First-time owners are always welcome.

04

Meet & Greet

If approved, we schedule an in-person or virtual meet-and-greet. This lets you and the pet make sure it's a great match before finalizing.

05

Finalize Adoption & Take Them Home

After a successful meet-and-greet, sign the adoption agreement and pay the adoption fee. Then take your new family member home!

06

Post-Adoption Support

Our team provides resources, tips, and check-ins to make sure both you and your pet are happy and thriving.

    Adoption Requirements

    Here's what you'll need to qualify for adoption.

    Be 18 or Older

    All applicants must be at least 18 years of age to adopt a pet.

    Pet-Friendly Home

    You must live in a space that allows pets. Renters should show landlord approval.

    Time Commitment

    Pets need time and attention daily. Please be honest about your schedule.

    Financial Readiness

    Owning a pet involves food, vet visits, and supplies. Be ready for the financial responsibility.

    Adoption Fees

    Our fees help cover veterinary care, vaccinations, and shelter costs.

    Dogs$150
    Cats$100

    All pets are vaccinated, microchipped, and spayed/neutered before adoption.
