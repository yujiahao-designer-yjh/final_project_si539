WhyPaws & Home?

We make adoption simple and supportive — especially for first-timers.

First-Timer Friendly

Our step-by-step process and resources are designed specifically for people adopting for the first time.

Carefully Matched

We help you find a pet that fits your lifestyle, living space, and experience level.

Ongoing Support

Our team is available to answer questions before, during, and after your adoption.

Health Guaranteed

All pets are vaccinated, spayed/neutered, and health-checked before adoption.
