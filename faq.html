About the Adoption Process

The process typically takes 3–7 days from application to bringing your pet home. After you submit your application, our team reviews it within 24–48 hours. If approved, we schedule a meet-and-greet, and adoption can be finalized shortly after.

Was this helpful?

Absolutely!Paws & Home was specifically designed with first-time owners in mind. We provide resources, guidance, and support throughout your adoption journey. We match pets to your experience level and lifestyle.

Was this helpful?

If your application is not approved, our team will let you know and provide feedback. In many cases, minor issues can be resolved and you can reapply. Our goal is to find the best home for each pet — not to turn people away.

Was this helpful?

Yes! Renters are welcome to apply. You will need to confirm that your landlord allows pets. We may ask for written proof of pet approval from your landlord before finalizing the adoption.

About the Pets

Yes. All pets are fully vaccinated, spayed or neutered, microchipped, and given a health check by a licensed veterinarian before listing. You receive a complete health record at adoption.

Was this helpful?

A pet with "Pending" status has an active application in review. You are still welcome to express interest — if the current application falls through, you may be next in line.

Was this helpful?

Yes! On the application, you can choose a specific pet you're interested in. You can also select "Open to any pet" and we'll help match you based on your lifestyle and answers.

Was this helpful?

Each pet has their own personality. Notes on each pet's profile describe how they get along with children, other pets, or specific environments. We also discuss this during the meet-and-greet.

Fees & Finances

Adoption fees are $150 for dogs and $100 for cats. These fees cover the cost of vaccinations, spay/neuter surgery, microchipping, and shelter care. Fees are paid when the adoption is finalized.

Was this helpful?

Ongoing costs include food ($30–$80/month), routine vet visits ($100–$300/year), supplies, and potential emergency medical costs. We recommend setting aside a small emergency fund for your pet's health care.

Was this helpful?

After Adoption

We offer post-adoption check-ins via email and phone for the first 30 days, plus a new pet owner guide with tips on feeding schedules, introducing your pet to a new home, vet recommendations, and training resources.

Was this helpful?

If the adoption doesn't work out within the first 30 days, please contact us. We will work with you to find a solution, which may include returning the pet to us. We never want a pet to be placed in a stressful situation.

Was this helpful?

We recommend preparing a few basics: a safe space (bed or crate), food and water bowls, pet-appropriate food, a leash or litter box, and removing any hazardous items from reach. We'll send you a complete checklist after your application is approved.
